Faculty of Computer Science and Orange, through Orange Educational Program, organizes the Spring School for students on topics such as: IoT, Network Communication Architectures, Virtualization Technologies, 5G Networks and their capabilities, Cyber Security. The Spring School activities are scheduled according to the program.


The program enables the students to put in practice the theoretical concepts learnt at the Faculty, acquiring, at the same time, technical skills, team-working abilities and improving their communication skills in a professional working environment.
