The Faculty of Computer Science organizes the Spring School for students on topics such as: IoT, Network Communication Architectures, Virtualization Technologies, 5G Networks and their capabilities, Cyber Security.


The Faculty of Computer Science and the Orange Educational Program were founders of this event in 2018.


The Spring School activities are scheduled between 10th and 13th April 2023, according to the program..

To be able to attend the activities, please register here.

The program enables the students to put in practice the theoretical concepts learnt at the Faculty, acquiring, at the same time, technical skills, team-working abilities and improving their communication skills in a professional working environment.
