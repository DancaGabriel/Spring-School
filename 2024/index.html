Spring School is an event founded in 2018 by the Applied Distributed Systems research group within the Faculty of Computer Science, "Alexandru Ioan Cuza" University of Ia»ôi and the Orange Educational Program. The event is dedicated to bachelor's/master's/doctorate students and teaching staff, from faculties with a technical profile, who will have the opportunity to acquire knowledge related to the field of Distributed Systems.
The program enables the students to put in practice the theoretical concepts learnt at the Faculty, acquiring, at the same time, technical skills, team-working abilities and improving their communication skills in a professional working environment.

Main Topics: Cloud/Edge Computing, Network Communication, Architectures, Virtualisation Technologies, 5G Networks, Security in Distributed Systems.


The Spring School activities are scheduled between 15th and 18th April 2024, according to the program.
