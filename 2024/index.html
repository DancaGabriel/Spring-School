The Spring School, inaugurated founded in 2018, is an initiative undertaken by the Applied Distributed Systems research group, housed within the Faculty of Computer Science, "Alexandru Ioan Cuza" University of Ia»ôi, in collaboration with the Orange Educational Program. This esteemed event is specifically dedicated to students at the bachelor's, master's, and doctorate levels, as well as academic staff, from technically oriented faculties. It offers a unique opportunity for participants to enhance their understanding and expertise in the realm of Distributed Systems.

The program provides students with the opportunity to apply theoretical concepts learned at the Faculty, while simultaneously developing technical abilities, teamwork skills, and enhancing their communication proficiency in a professional working environment.

Main Topics: Cloud/Edge Computing, Network Communication Architectures, Virtualisation Technologies, 5G Networks, Security in Distributed Systems.


The Spring School activities are scheduled between 15th and 18th April 2024, according to the program.
